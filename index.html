Truck types

DRY VAN
We specialize in dispatching Dry Van, Reefer, and Flatbed with brand new MC Authority. We have extensive contacts with brokers who specialize in Fresh MCs, and we can help you get started. Trailer year 2012 and above. 53ft with the swing doors.
FLATBED
We specialize in dispatching Dry Van, Reefer, and Flatbed with brand new MC Authority. We have extensive contacts with brokers who specialize in Fresh MCs, and we can help you get started. 48ft and 53ft only.
REEFER
We specialize in dispatching Dry Van, Reefer, and Flatbed with brand new MC Authority. We have extensive contacts with brokers who specialize in Fresh MCs, and we can help you get started. Trailer year 2012 and above. 53ft with the swing doors.
STEP-DECK
We specialize in dispatching Dry Van, Reefer, Step-Deck and Flatbed with brand new MC Authority. We have extensive contacts with brokers who specialize in Fresh MCs, and we can help you get started. 53ft only.
Power only
We work with a minimum of 6 months old active MC# Authority for Power Only. You will need to Trailer Interchange or non-owned insurance. 5 ratchets traps and 5 chains and binders. PO is open to all the states. The driver should be ok with driver-assist whenever required at the shipper or the receiver. PO should be open to all kinds of loads i.e. *Hook and drop *Loadout trailer *Empty trailer moves *Round trips *Loaded Flatbed trailers *Tow away Trailers T & C apply.
BOX TRUCKS
We specialize in dispatching Cargo Vans, Box trucks. We are here to help our clients get more money. We do this by searching for prospective hotshot loads on different platforms – some of which may not be accessible or recognized by people trying to arrange loads for themselves.
